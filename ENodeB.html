EnodeB

eNodeB (Evolved Node B) systems are the critical foundation of LTE-based railway communication networks, ensuring high-speed, reliable, and secure wireless connectivity for operational and passenger services. Our eNodeB solutions are specifically engineered for mission-critical railway environments, offering advanced features such as QoS (Quality of Service) prioritization, handover management, low latency communication, and high data throughput. Built to withstand harsh outdoor conditions, our eNodeB units support both fixed and mobile deployments, enabling continuous coverage across stations, tracks, tunnels, and yards.


We offer complete eNodeB solutions including site survey, network design, supply, installation, commissioning, optimization, and maintenance services. Our products comply with 3GPP standards for LTE and are designed for integration with EPC (Evolved Packet Core) and railway-specific applications like mission-critical push-to-talk (MCPTT), video surveillance, and real-time data services. With scalable configurations and robust cybersecurity features, our eNodeB systems are tailored to meet the future-ready communication needs of modern railway operations.


  • High-performance eNodeB systems for reliable, low-latency LTE communication across railway infrastructures.

  • End-to-end services from network design, equipment supply, deployment, to optimization and maintenance.
